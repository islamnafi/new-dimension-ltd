About Us

Equipping Bangladesh for Tomorrow

The firm started its journey in July 2023. The firm is involved in the import and supply of various items, especially vehicles, equipment, Machines, Spare parts raw materials etc. The firm is working with military and Paramilitary organizations like DGDP (Army, Navy, Airforce), CMTD, Bangladesh Ordnance Factories, Bangladesh Police, RAB, BGB, Bangladesh Fire Service & Civil Defence and different ministries of the Bangladesh Govt. The firm is also working with the Garments sector of Bangladesh.

  • Comprehensive engineering, installation, and project acceptance
  • Worldwide sourcing with fast, reliable logistics
  • Expertise in defense, paramilitary, and government procurement
